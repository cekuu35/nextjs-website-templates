# Demo Content and Client-Delivery Checklist

Use this checklist for every template before presenting it as finished client work.

## Identity and claims

- Replace the demo business name, logo, favicon, domain, email, phone, address, and social links.
- Remove placeholder testimonials, ratings, client logos, awards, counters, statistics, certifications, and press mentions.
- Verify service descriptions, credentials, team biographies, case studies, prices, availability, and guarantees with the client.
- Replace all stock/demo imagery and confirm usage rights.

## Functional integrations

- Connect forms to the client's approved backend or form provider.
- Add server-side validation, spam protection, consent records, success/error states, and delivery monitoring.
- Replace demo booking, reservation, cart, checkout, download, authentication, and newsletter behavior with real services where required.
- Test notification recipients, reply-to behavior, confirmation emails, failure handling, rate limits, and abuse controls.

## Legal, privacy, and accessibility

- Obtain client-approved privacy, cookie, terms, refund, cancellation, medical/legal disclaimers, and regulatory copy.
- Configure analytics and consent behavior for the client's jurisdiction.
- Check keyboard navigation, focus states, headings, labels, alternative text, contrast, reduced motion, and screen-reader behavior.

## Quality assurance

- Review every route, button, link, image, metadata field, structured-data entry, and error page.
- Test representative phones, tablets, desktop widths, and supported browsers.
- Run type-check, lint, production build, and dependency/security review.
- Verify performance budgets, image optimization, caching, redirects, canonical URLs, sitemap, robots rules, and social previews.
- Test the production domain, SSL, environment variables, monitoring, backups, and rollback plan.

## Client sign-off

- Record the exact scope and integrations delivered.
- Have the client approve content, factual claims, legal copy, analytics, and launch behavior.
- Keep a clean handoff copy plus deployment and service-account documentation.
